From August 2017 until February 2019 I was a camera operator and videographer for the UConn Athletics Department. I filmed every sport including but not limited to Basketball, Field Hockey, and Soccer.

During my time there I learned how to operate professional grade cameras in order to broadcast, live stream, or create highlights from the games that were being filmed.

I got hired at the University of Wollongong to be a videographer for the men's soccer team. I filmed their weekly games and then created highlights from the footage and uploaded the videos onto Youtube for the players, fans, and students to watch and enjoy.
