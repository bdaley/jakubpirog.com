My Story

Hi.

My name is Jakub Pirog.

I am a first generation Polish-American citizen whose parents immigrated to the U.S. from Poland in the early '90s. From a young age, they taught me the values of patience and hard work which have been instrumental in the making of my professional career.

My ambitions of becoming a photographer have taken me across the globe, given me the privilege of working with some truly unforgettable people, and have opened my eyes to a world of amazing opportunities. I am tremendously thankful for everything I have been able to experience so far. On the right side of the screen, you can see some of my favorite photos that I have captured along my journey.

Currently, I make graphics and run outreach for SUBOG (@subogatuconn), intern for an emmy award winning film company (Triple Threat TV), and am also leading a team of students to produce a video for a startup company called FoodDock.
